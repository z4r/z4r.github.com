Usefull _shortcut_ in __vimdiff__:

- `do` - _diff obtain_
- `dp` - _diff put_
- `]c` - _jump forward_
- `[c` - _jump backward_
- `zo` - _open block_
- `zc` - _close block_
- `Ctrl W + Ctrl W` - _change window_
